Task Breakdown

  1. Parametrically design plant clips in Grasshopper and Rhino
  2. Laser cut plant clips
  3. Parametrically design moss pole pieces in Grasshopper and Rhino
  4. 3D print moss pole pieces
  5. Stretch Goal: Design a 3D printed mold in Rhino for casting a silicone mold using meshes from Thingiverse
  6. Stretch Goal: Cast decorative moss pole caps that can be attached to the top of the moss poles

Stretch goals listed in this section and below are not expected to be achieved 100%. Deviations or incompletion of the stretch goals should not result in point deductions. Rather they are goals that I hope I will have time to complete as "extras" or "nice to haves" for the final assignment.

Timeline + Contingency Plan

Timeline Task Plan Contingency Plan
by 11/25 Parametrically design moss pole pieces and clips in Grasshopper and Rhino All of the moss pole pieces and the clips will be designed parametrically in Grasshopper so that sizes can be easily adjusted. The moss pole pieces will use a screw mechanism to attach to each other
  • If some of the moss pole pieces prove too dificult to model parametrically in Grasshopper, I will model them in Rhino (and these pieces will therefore not be parametric)
  • The clips will be easy to design in Rhino and so if the schedule starts to slip, the clips can be designed in Rhino rather than parametrically
  • If the screw mechanism for the moss pole pieces is too difficult or requires too much time, I will design an interference fit or clipping mechanism instead
